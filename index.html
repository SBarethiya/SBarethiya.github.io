About

   

I am a PhD candidate in Computational Chemistry at UMass Amherst.     My work combines molecular modeling, molecular dynamics, and machine learning to solve biological problems.

 
 
   

Research

    •      
  • Biophysics based variant effect predictor — combing the physics-based features with CNN and GCN models.
